💙 You're Not Alone

Feeling lonely happens — but brighter moments are ahead. Here’s what might help:

  • 📞 Reach out to a friend or family member, even if it's just a short message.
  • 🎵 Play your favorite upbeat playlist or relaxing music.
  • 📖 Pick up a good book or watch a feel-good movie.
  • ☀ Step outside for some fresh air — a short walk can shift your mood.
  • 📝 Write down three good things about yourself or your day, no matter how small.
  • 🌸 Remember: tough days don’t last, and you are valued more than you realize.
